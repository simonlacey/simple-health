Who we are. What we are. What we do.

"Insurance shouldn’t cost you an arm and a leg and put in the poor house.
We get that."

We know what it’s like. You need insurance, you want insurance, but you’ve got bills to pay, and your money only goes so far.

Health insurance can be a major hassle. Coverages, premiums, copays, deductibles. It’s all so confusing and time consuming. And then, how do you know that a plan is the right one for you.

We’re to help you get the coverage you need. The plan that fits your health insurance needs and your budget. And where here to make the entire buying experience quick, easy and painless.

Our Core Values

Simple Health has assembled a diverse portfolio of superior health insurance products from leading health insurance carriers, each carefully selected based on its ability to provide exceptional value and coverage to our customers.

Our strength and success is directly linked to our people and technology. We have a team of highly experienced trained insurance professionals, all focused on providing our customers with the very best service. We also embrace technological innovation, which us has allowed us to develop powerful proprietary sales and quoting systems. Our team is continually improving and updating our systems and processes so that we may better serve our customers.

In November 2015 Health Benefits Center officially became Simple Health Insurance. The name change was part of a larger corporate rebranding initiative that was designed to make the company more readily identifiable to consumers by easily and quickly establishing our core focus, products and services.

Simple Insurance is always looking to the future. We are dedicated to helping our customers through ever changing times and connecting them to the coverage that best fits their needs and builds security for them and their families.
